EPIC Club Logo

Logo created for the Electronics, Process Instrumentation and Computer Club at Del Tech University. Visual mark combines elements that represent the analog space of electronics and digital space of computer language inside a process of iteration and refinement. Typography is informed by the brackets in the visual logo, and by the binary language to further unify the analog and digital identities of the club.
