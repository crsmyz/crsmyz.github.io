MenuDrive

MenuDrive is an internet food ordering product that helps restaurants prepare, deliver and track orders all online. I designed various materials for the marketing and support of this product which includes product flyers, postcards, email blasts, ebook covers, a full ebook layout, benefit flyers and a PowerPoint layout.

Responsibilities

  1. Flyer design
  2. Postcard design
  3. eBook layout and typography
  4. Email design and HTML
  5. PowerPoint templates
  6. Client banners
  7. Client brand integration with product
