About Me

My name is Chris Medykiewicz, I'm designer based around Philadelphia. I am skilled at creating exceptional design, development and marketing solutions for my clients and employers. I am driven by a passion and love for design, development and problem solving. Through my passion and design savvy I iterate down to the best solution for a given project. My process is inclusive end to end from inception to product launch. I start with client and user research to initialize concepts, then design and develop, iterate through user testing, and product launch.
