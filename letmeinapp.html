LetMeIn App

A quick and easy way to notify you when to let your pet indoors, and a convenient pet tracker so you can keep tabs on where your pet has been and its current location. Uses GPS and smart collar integration to notify you on the phone or smartwatch platforms.

Responsibilities

  1. Information architecture
  2. Personas
  3. Product map
  4. User flows
  5. Page layout
  6. Icon design
  7. Logo design

Goals

  • Create an effective way to track outdoor free roaming pets.
  • Enable a notification system to let pet owners know when to let their pets inside.
  • Give pet owners a history of where their pet has been, and infer outdoor activities.

Challenges

  • Building a small reliable and accurate pet tracking transmitter that uses GPS, NFC and Bluetooth technology.
  • Determining pets activities outdoors based on just geolocation data.
  • Notifications based on proximity of pet to the owners house and how house size and architecture can be compensated for.

Solutions

  • Use current smartphone technology and current suppliers that are in high demand that utilize the same technology.
  • There was no real need to judge what pet was or wasn't doing outdoors to any real end for the user.
  • Users are given an option to set a distance and an idle time for the pet in order to determine if the pet is actually waiting to be let in. Two data points gives this feature less room for when the pet is close to the house or if the outdoor area is small.
