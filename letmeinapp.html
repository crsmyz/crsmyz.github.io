LetMeIn App

A quick and easy way to notify you when to let your pet indoors, and a convenient pet tracker so you can keep tabs on where your pet has been and its current location. Uses GPS and smart collar integration to notify you on the phone or smartwatch devices.

Responsibilities

  1. Information architecture
  2. Personas
  3. Product map
  4. User flows
  5. Page layout
  6. Icon design
  7. Logo design

Goals

  • Create an effective and easy way to track outdoor free roaming pets.
  • Easily find and retrieve lost pets.
  • Cut down on the amount of time a pet is waiting at the door.
  • Give pet owners a visual history of where their pet has been.

Challenges

  • App must be paired with a smart collar with GPS in order for features to work.
  • Home size variance and variable distance between devices for door notifications could cause an annoying amount of interruption.

Solutions

  • Creat a modular collar that can fit a variety of outdoor pets and animals equipped with GPS, NFC and Bluetooth capability.
  • After a specific amount of notifications user is asked they want to increase the notification distance between devices.
