Glassbox App

Glassbox is a legislative bill tracking app that gives users information on how legislation moves through the government and details about what the legislation contains. It allows users to customize the legislation they see by choosing interests that are relevant to them and integrated communication tools allow users to give feedback and thoughts on legislation directly to lawmakers.

Responsibilities

  1. UX design
  2. Product design
  3. UI design

Goals

  • Enable users to easily track legislation stats, history and position inside of the government
  • Help users filter, navigate, read and understand all forms of legislation moving through government
  • Give users tools for easy and effective communication with lawmakers over legislation and issues

Challenges

  • User attention span may not be captured by any given piece of legislation due to the length of Bill and the langague used in the content
  • Only politically inclined people will be compelled to use the app at the expense of non politically motivated people
  • Large pieces of legislation are clunky and difficult to read on a small mobile device
  • Legislation contains jargon and legal language that can be hard to understand for regular people
  • Challenging to find relevant information within legislation for the user and this adds a barrier to productive commuincation with lawmakers

Solutions

  • User interest filtering, Bill summaries, ease of lawmaker communication, subdivided legislation sections and in app legal definitions provide a quicker way of digesting a massive amount of information
  • User interest filtering, Bill summary and ease of communication between user and lawmaker allows for non politically inclined users to easily interact without needing to read any Bills
  • Subdivide legislation in smaller chunks based on legislative writing standards so information can be understood while looking through the Bill for details
  • Use in app word highlighter to allow for quick definition look ups for legal language
  • Allow for users to pick interest areas that filter out Bills they aren't interested in and subdivide legislation into smaller pieces based on legislative writing standards so users can find information faster
