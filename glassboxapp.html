Glassbox App

Glassbox is a legislative bill tracking app that gives users information on how legislation moves through the government and details about what the legislation contains. It allows users to customize the legislation they see by choosing interests that are relevant to them and integrated communication tools allow users to give feedback and thoughts on legislation directly to lawmakers.

Responsibilities

  1. UX design
  2. Product design
  3. UI design

Goals

  • Make tracking legislature through the government easier
  • Help common people understand legislature
  • Enable common people to become involved in the lawmaking process by showing support or disapproval for laws

Challenges

  • Attention constraints
  • Political interest
  • Effect on congress
  • Large bills on phones

Solutions

  • Only show information that gives an idea of what is going on. Allow the user to seek out more info
  • Make presentation of the information straight forward and easy
  • Encourage and enable easy communication with good in app tools between users and congress persons.
  • Sub divide bills into pieces that matter to the users, allowing them to quickly read and get to pieces of the legislation that matter to them.
